04.04.01. Design sprint: Native app

One of the final assignments for this semester. A group work, where we had to create a native app - a learning management tool of our dreams. We had no frames, only our own imagination, a complete freedom as UX designers. It was a lot of fun to brainstorm and come up with any kind of ideas, for example how to implement the well known app “Tinder” in our learning tool.
We came up with the name BUTTER - and a slogan: “USE BUTTER, TO BE BETTER!” We created a XD prototype and in the end presented it with a 7 minute pitch.
To write a pitch was a challenge - to find the aspects that would be the most interesting for our audience, to find points why people would like to use our app.
